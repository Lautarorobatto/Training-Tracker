✅ General Rules
Weigh at the same time every day
Same scale, always in the same fixed spot on tile or hardwood
Underwear only — same every single time
Replicate the exact same conditions every weigh-in
Consistency matters more than the scale itself — you're tracking a trend, not a single number.
🌅 Morning Weigh-In
Immediately after waking up
After using the bathroom
Fully fasted — no water, food, or coffee beforehand
Before any workout — fluid loss deflates the number
Your most reliable reading — fasted, rested, and free from daily variables.
🌙 Evening Weigh-In
Right before bed — make it the last thing you do
After using the bathroom
At least 2 hours after your last meal
Not right after training or a shower — results will be skewed
The AM→PM delta shows how food, water, and activity accumulate through the day.
